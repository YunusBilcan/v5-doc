---
title: Konumlandırma (Position)
---

Bir elementi konumlandırmak için sizler için önceden tanımlanmış classları kullanabilirsiniz.

```html
<div class="position-static">...</div>
<div class="position-relative">...</div>
<div class="position-absolute">...</div>
<div class="position-fixed">...</div>
<div class="position-sticky">...</div>
```

### Ögeleri konumlandırma

##### position: absolute;

<div class="v5-example-position-utils rounded bg-gray position-relative mb-2">
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute top-0 left-0">
        1
    </div>
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute top-0 right-0">
        2
    </div>
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute top-50 left-50">
        3
    </div>
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute bottom-50 right-50">
        4
    </div>
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute bottom-0 left-0">
        5
    </div>
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute bottom-0 right-0">
        6
    </div>
</div>

```html
<div class="position-absolute top-0 left-0"> 1 </div> 
<div class="position-absolute top-0 right-0"> 2 </div> 
<div class="position-absolute top-50 left-50"> 3 </div> 
<div class="position-absolute bottom-50 right-50"> 4 </div> 
<div class="position-absolute bottom-0 left-0"> 5 </div> 
<div class="position-absolute bottom-0 right-0"> 6 </div>
```

### Merkezi konumlandırma

##### transform: translate(X, Y);

<div class="v5-example-position-utils rounded bg-gray position-relative mb-2">
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute top-0 left-0 translate-middle">
        1
    </div>
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute top-0 left-100 translate-middle">
        2
    </div>
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute top-50 left-50 translate-middle">
        3
    </div>
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute bottom-50 right-50 translate-middle">
        4
    </div>
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute top-100 left-0 translate-middle">
        5
    </div>
    <div class="v5-example-position bg-primary text-white rounded position-absolute top-100 left-100 translate-middle">
        6
    </div>
</div>

```html
<div class="position-absolute top-0 left-0 translate-middle"> 1 </div> 
<div class="position-absolute top-0 left-100 translate-middle"> 2 </div> 
<div class="position-absolute top-50 left-50 translate-middle"> 3 </div> 
<div class="position-absolute bottom-50 right-50 translate-middle"> 4 </div> 
<div class="position-absolute top-100 left-0 translate-middle"> 5 </div> 
<div class="position-absolute top-100 left-100 translate-middle"> 6 </div>
```

#### Örnekler

<div class="d-flex justify-content-around mb-2">
  <button class="btn btn-primary position-relative">
    Mesajlarım <span class="position-absolute top-0 left-100 translate-middle badge bg-dark">+9</span>
  </button>
  <button class="btn btn-dark position-relative ml-4">
    İpucu <span class="position-absolute top-0 left-100 translate-middle badge border border-light border-circle bg-danger p-1"></span>
  </button>
</div>

```html
<button class="btn btn-primary position-relative">
  Mesajlarım 
  <span class="position-absolute top-0 left-100 translate-middle badge bg-dark">+9</span>
</button>

<button class="btn btn-dark position-relative ml-4">
  İpucu

```
